Tina Mechleb Gets Her First Dog

Thinking of adopting a pet? DOGGO has many loving, beautiful dogs that are waiting for you. Our facilities count around 500 dogs. Most of these pets have had a bad past, and they are waiting for a second chance at life. All our animals are followed by vets: they are vaccinated, de-wormed and spayed/neutered. Whether you want an energetic puppy who loves to run around and play, a senior dog who wants to snuggle and sleep by your side, we can help you contact us.
